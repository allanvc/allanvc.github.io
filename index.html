About Me

I am a Statistician and an R developer during my free time. I am currently the Lead Data Scientist at the National Fund for Educational Development (FNDE) in Brazil. You can find more about me and my projects on my Resume and my GitHub.

Research Interest

My main interests are Statistical Computing, Optimization, Mathematical Finance, Stochastic Processes, and Astrostatistics.
