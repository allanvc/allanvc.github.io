Round Table - Data Analysis in the Public Sector

Allan Quadros
Jul 3, 2019
Slides

Abstract

The adoption of Statistics and Data Science in the public sector is increasing in Brazil. However, public agents still do not know how those fields can help them in decision making and other activities. This talk aimed to walk the audience through the opportunities and benefits of using Statistics and Data Science in the Government.
